iHP Tool

The brand was set up in 1995. Our experience in the industries stretched way back to the early 70s. Our main business is to source and supply industrial equipments to the construction, shipbuilding, oil explorations, mining, manufacturing and general industries.

iHP Tools products have gained the trust and confidence of our valued customers, to provide more than just comprehensive and reliable equipments. We hold the highest aspect for SAFETY to our valued customers.

Brevini
